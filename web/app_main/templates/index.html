SARAH FAIRLIE

Fairlie designed and built the camera mount for the Wigglegif Photobooth.

She is the Co-Artistic Director of Caterwaul Theatre and a member of the Smalltalk Collective. She also takes photos and makes animation, projections and shadow puppets.

Upcoming projects include installations at HarbourKids Snowglobe at the HarbourFront Centre and the Live Art Party as part of the Progress Festival at the Theatre Centre.

SARAH FRIEND

Sarah Friend is an interdisciplinary designer and developer. For the Wigglegif Photobooth, she put together software to interact with the cameras and the web app backend.

By day she is the production manager and senior designer at Guild Eyewear, as well as the Founder of Small Talk, an Interdisciplinary Lecture Series.

Her current research interests include: abstract and educational games, sustainable manufacturing, and issues surrounding cryptography and net neutrality.

Many thanks to Rod Fitzsimmons-Frey for camera-loaning, chdk-related code, and so much support and advice.

www.sarahfriend.com

KAYLA LEWIS



Kayla Lewis is the designer and front-end developer for the Wigglegif Photobooth website.

Her interests include education, ecotechnology, and resource-based economics.

She enjoys exploring web technologies in her free time and is currently working on her second degree in Information Security.
